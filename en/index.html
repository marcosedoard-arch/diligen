  • The mandate letter PDF metadata were generated by a non-corporate tool, with a generic personal email.

How fast do I get the verdict?
X-Ray Express delivers in 48h; Complete in 5 to 7 business days. For urgent decisions inside a 48–72h window, start with Express and escalate to EDD if a relevant indicator appears.
What about confidentiality and the LGPD?
We operate under Brazil's LGPD, with data minimization and a fraud-prevention legal basis. The engagement is confidential, the intake channel is secure, and we never expose real personal data in reports or materials.
Is it worth the price?
A poultry container is worth tens of thousands of dollars; a trial-order advance tops USD 100k. The report is a fraction of that. You're not paying for an expense — you're paying for insurance against losing the cargo, the money and your name.
What exactly do I get?
A clear verdict — GO, NO-GO or EDD — with the list of red flags, the confidence level (High/Medium/Low) per finding and an evidence annex with dated screenshot, hash and source. Report in PT and/or EN.
How do I send the documents?
Through the secure channel we send after your request. You give us the counterparty's name and registry plus the documents you received (mandate, ICPO, SCO, NCNDA, proposal). The more material, the deeper we go.
Do you guarantee you'll find the fraud?
We don't promise to "find fraud" or recover money — that would be dishonest and out of our control. Our guarantee is honest: if the report doesn't cover the points on our verification checklist, you don't pay.
